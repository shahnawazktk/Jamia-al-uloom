Our Introduction

Jamia Ul Uloom stands as a beacon of Islamic knowledge and spiritual guidance, committed to producing scholars who are well-versed in both traditional Islamic sciences and contemporary understanding.

Founded in 1995, our institution has grown from a small madrasa with just 20 students to a comprehensive Islamic university serving over 450 students. We pride ourselves on maintaining the authenticity of traditional Islamic education while adapting to the needs of the modern world.

Our campus features modern classrooms, a well-stocked library with over 5,000 books, computer labs, and spacious prayer halls. We believe in providing a holistic educational experience that nurtures the mind, body, and soul.

  • Government Registered Institution
  • Recognized by Islamic Universities
  • Experienced and Qualified Faculty
  • Modern Educational Facilities
  • Comprehensive Curriculum
  • Focus on Character Building

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive Islamic education that produces knowledgeable, pious, and socially responsible individuals who can contribute positively to society while upholding the values of Islam.

  • Preserve and transmit authentic Islamic knowledge
  • Develop moral and ethical character
  • Foster intellectual growth and critical thinking
  • Serve the community through education

Our Vision

To be a leading Islamic educational institution that produces scholars and individuals who are not only well-versed in Islamic sciences but also capable of addressing contemporary challenges with wisdom and knowledge.

  • Excellence in Islamic education
  • Integration of traditional and modern knowledge
  • Global recognition and impact

Programs Offered

Comprehensive Islamic education programs

Dars-e-Nizami

An 8-year comprehensive program covering Islamic jurisprudence, theology, Arabic literature, and Quranic sciences.

  • • Duration: 8 Years
  • • Degree: Shahadat-ul-Alimiya
  • • Subjects: Fiqh, Hadith, Tafseer, Arabic

Hifz-ul-Quran

Complete memorization of the Holy Quran with proper Tajweed and pronunciation.

  • • Duration: 2-3 Years
  • • Degree: Shahadat-ul-Hifz
  • • Focus: Memorization & Tajweed

Arabic Language

Intensive Arabic language program focusing on reading, writing, and speaking skills.

  • • Duration: 2 Years
  • • Degree: Arabic Proficiency
  • • Focus: Grammar & Literature

Tajweed & Qira'at

Learn the proper pronunciation and recitation of the Holy Quran.

  • • Duration: 1-2 Years
  • • Certificate: Tajweed Certification
  • • Focus: Pronunciation & Rules

Junior Classes

Foundation classes for children focusing on basic Islamic education and Quran reading.

  • • Age: 5-12 Years
  • • Focus: Basics of Islam
  • • Includes: Nazra, Kalimas, Duas

Online Classes

Flexible online learning options for students who cannot attend physically.

  • • Mode: Online (Zoom/Skype)
  • • Courses: Various
  • • Flexible Timing
